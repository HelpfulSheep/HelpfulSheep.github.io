instructions

the aim of the game is to make the snake grow by eating the food and the small defenseless creatures

avoid crashing into the walls or into your own tail at all costs

keep in mind that the snake cannot stop or move backwards

gameplay

you may select the game type and the game level from the top panel

use the four squares or arrow keys to change direction

you may change the background color by activating the color picker

problems

this is more or less an experimental version and might not work properly on some devices or browsers

if that is the case, please consider trying out the original version on your pc or mac

about the game

the game was written as a tribute to the original snake game found on old nokia phones

the original kicked ass and i would like to thank its creators for what i believe to be the best snake game of all times
