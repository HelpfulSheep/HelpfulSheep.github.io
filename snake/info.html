instructions

the aim of the game is to make the snake grow by eating the food and the small defenseless creatures

avoid crashing into the walls or into your own tail at all costs

keep in mind that the snake cannot stop or move backwards

gameplay

you may select the game type and the game level from the left panel

the game levels range from wormingly slow to squirrelly fast

use the arrow keys to change direction

you may use f2 to start a new game and esc to pause or resume the current game

you may change the background color by activating the color picker

clicking on more gives you a list of all the submitted scores

houston, we have a problem
